Latex Curriculum Vitae is a simple solution for writing job applications with producing beautiful documents with LaTEX. You can write, compile and send the stuff directly within the app. You need just a Windows 10 Computer and a basic knowledge of LaTEX all other dependencies are bundled by the release.

Features

Write automated high quality job applications

Latex Curriculum Vitae supports out of the box automated writing of Job Applications. It uses for rendering and producing the PDFs a prepared bunch of LaTEX files.

Storing User information

You just have to enter your name, address and other personal information just once while the first run. LaTEX uses each iteration this stored information. If you want to change anything, just use the implemented Window to manage and update your information.

Really fast producing

Once modifed the delivered Latex-files or placed your own, Latex Curriculum Vitae uses it for generating new applications.

Direct Email or Upload

In case of writing to a company via email, just enter the email address in the apps email field. Directly after finishing the compiling process by LaTEX, Latex Curriculum Vitae sends the email out to the company, with you in BCC. If you need stuff for uploading your documents to companies application form, just leave the email field empty and Windows Explorer starts and shows your document for uploading.

Managing the state of art

Latex Curriculum Vitae provides a export of all application data to a CSV file which is readable with the most calc programs. Also it is a database implemented, where you can manage the state of the art.

Localized

Latex Curriculum Vitae is available in English and German.
